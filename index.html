A little about me

I became interested in web design way back in 1998, when I worked for a small electronics company who had no web site presence. Of course, at the time web sites very rudimentary and had to be hand coded. I created a huge online catalog presence for this company and found I could work for hours and not even be phased. I loved it. I did not know anything, so I had to teach myself from scratch and back then there was not even css, or least not that I knew, so lots and lots of tables. :-). After that company closed in 2008 due to economic hit, I went back to school for web design. Received a certificate, and then never worked in the field. I was intimated and suffered from imposter syndrome enough to keep me out of the job market. Fast forward to now and I am back, trying to learn all the skills I did not back in 2010.

Just completed a Front-End Development course (July 2022) that included dev tools,programs and frameworks including HTML/CSS/JavaScript/Sass/NPM/Vue/Yarn and Nuxt.
